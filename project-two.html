RightBite Cafeteria App

The RightBite Cafeteria app is a mobile app that connects with campus cafeterias and students allowing them to purchase meals from their desks. After purchasing they are able to pick up their meals without waiting in long lineups.

Visual Design

RightBite Cafeteria was made with the purpose of adding efficiency to students lives. The overall design is minimal, clean and easy to navigate through. It displays clean related visuals to make their experience more entertaining.
