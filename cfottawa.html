CF Ottawa Website Design

The Canadian Culinary Federation (CCFC) was founded in 1963, and became part of Ottawa as a non-profit organization in 1972. The purpose of redesigning CF Ottawa's website is to expand their membership base, to attract a younger audience of chefs, as well as corporate members who will partner with them for events and sponsorships.

Project Roles

Delphine Sullivan: Rebranding lead, web designer/developer

Diandra Thompson: Web designer/developer

Award of Excellence

2nd Place Award of Excellence for the Culinary Federation Ottawa Rebrand. Awarded on December 4th, 2018 at REACTION Applied Research Showcase located on the Algonquin College Woodroffe campus.

Visual Design

Online presence should have a marked consistency, and follow brand guidelines across all platforms. Content should be clean, minimal, and easy to read. Illustrations were designed by Delphine Sullivan.
