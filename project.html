MGFX Website Design

MGFX is a SAAS website. A software as a service where users can access its software with a subscription, rather than a one-time purchase.

Iconography

The overall design of the icons are simple using thin strokes. A simple design perfectly outlines a straightforward message and guides the user the steps of what represents what.

Open Sans Condensed

Open Sans Condensed is a humanist sans serif typeface. It was designed with an upright stress, open forms and a neutral, yet friendly appearance. It's used as the body copy. It is used for headings and subheadings
