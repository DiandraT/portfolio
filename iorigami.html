iOrigami App

iOrigami is a paper folding craft app. You can create origami decorations by using paper combined with techniques and abilities iOrigami teaches you. In this application we give multiple instructions from beginner level to advanced, which you can use as references to create and share your origami decoration.

Visual Design

Since origami making is such a complex craft — the app was created with the purpose of a straightforward appearance by using minimal and interesting design elements. Users can easily follow without confusion and overwhelming design detail because of its cohesive simplicity.
